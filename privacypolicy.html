El Monty Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

Your Data


El Monty stores your phrases, notes, and categories locally on your device.

El Monty does not require an account and does not upload your personal phrase library to any server.

Speech Recognition

When you choose to use voice capture, El Monty uses Apple's Speech Recognition services.

Speech recognition is provided by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Translation

When you request a translation, El Monty uses Apple's Translation framework.

Translation requests are handled by Apple according to Apple's privacy practices.

Analytics

El Monty does not collect analytics.

Advertising

El Monty does not display advertisements.

Data Sharing

El Monty does not sell or share your personal data.
