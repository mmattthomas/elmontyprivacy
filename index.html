Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 2026

Your Data

El Monty stores your phrases, translations, notes, categories, and review information locally on your device.

El Monty does not require an account and does not upload your personal phrase library to an El Monty server.

Speech Recognition

When you choose to use voice capture, El Monty uses Apple's speech-recognition services to convert your spoken words into text.

Speech recognition is provided by Apple and is subject to Apple's applicable privacy practices.

Translation

When you request a translation, El Monty uses Apple's Translation framework to provide the translated text.

Translation functionality is provided by Apple and is subject to Apple's applicable privacy practices.

Analytics and Advertising

El Monty does not currently use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

El Monty does not sell your personal information. El Monty does not share your personal phrase library with advertisers or data brokers.

Language Packs

El Monty may offer downloadable language packs in the future. The app may contact a server to retrieve pack descriptions and phrase content. Personal phrases and notes will not be included in those requests unless this policy is updated and the app clearly informs you.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as El Monty gains new features. The updated date at the top of this page will identify the latest version.
